markup

/ˈmɑːkʌp/
noun
noun: markup; plural noun: markups; noun: mark-up; plural noun: mark-ups
  1. the amount added to the cost price of goods to cover overheads and profit.
    a mark up of 50 per cent
  2. the process or result of correcting text in preparation for printing.
  3. computing
    a set of tags assigned to elements of a text to indicate their to the rest of the text or dedicate how they should be displayed.
    markup languages
